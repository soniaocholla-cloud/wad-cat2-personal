I interviewed Allan and Douglas, employess at Layali Café, which is located along Mai Mahiu Road at Magharibi Place. During the interview, i asked them about the purpose of the café and the kind of website they would like to have. They explained that their main goal is to serve and satisfy customers by providing qualityfood, refreshing beverages and excellent customer service. Their target audeinece is everyone, but they especially serve students because the café is located near student residences and learning institutions. They mentioned that the msot information to be displayed on their website is their menu, particularly popular items such as their Lotus biscoff waffles, scud and milkshakes. They also wanted the website to showcase the café's warm and welcoming atmosphere through photographs of their interior, exterior , decor and customers enjoying their time at the café. Finally, they emphasized that visitors should be able to contact them easily through phone calls, Whatsapp, Instagram and Tiktok. These communication options therefore included in the website design.
